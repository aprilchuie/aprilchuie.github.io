Hippo Time

Worked with a team of undergraduate students and faculty from the University of California, Irvine to integrate biofeedback into a video game, to teach children with autism spectrum disorder proper deep breathing techniques to deal wtih everyday stressors.

I was a student programmer, so I helped program individual scenes in Unity to incorporate in the Hippo Time game. I also took part in recording and keeping track of progress made on the video game using Bitbucket and TeamGantt.
